Design Matters x Digital Photography

10.6.16


What is the future of digital photography in design? Some predict that photography was just a stepping stone for what is next to come. Stephen Mayes from Time Magazine claims “It’s time to stop talking about photography. It’s not that photography is dead as many have claimed, but it’s gone.” But the question still remains. What is this next evolutionary step for photography? After a couple recent years of smartphones revolutionizing the way we take, consume and communicate with pictures, we’re only starting to grasp the consequences — and opportunities — that new technology brings to photography. Severin Matusek, VP Community at EyeEm claims that Virtual Reality and what VR can offer are set to enter the stage louder than ever and redefine what photography can do in 2016.
