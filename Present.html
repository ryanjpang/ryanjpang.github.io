Design Matters x Digital Photography


Photography and Web Design go hand-in-hand. With the high demand of photographers to take photos for their website, many web designers have decided to take on the art of photography themselves. Although it may be tough to learn, as long as the designer has the eye for photography, learning to utilize the camera can be done with a bit of practice. However having the eye for photography, lighting and composition may be more difficult to obtain.



The famous cliché “A picture is worth a 1,000 words” translated in web design can mean the picture that ended you up with an additional 1,000 leads, 1,000 likes, 1,000 new subscribers, 1,000 sales or campaigns. According to Anne Pick, her words exactly, “Depending upon how well it’s done, 1,000 may or may not be an exaggeration”. Anne also believes the elements of your marketing and design combined tells your brand’s story. She and many other designers believe that the use of good photography on your website may be the tipping point of the success of your website.
